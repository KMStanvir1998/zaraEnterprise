Who we are

Established in January 2000, Zara Enterprise is a distinguished provider of Facilities Management services. Our commitment to delivering highly effective and customized solutions has earned us a reputation for excellence in the industry. At Zara Enterprise, we pride ourselves on our unwavering dedication to exceptional customer service. Currently, we specialize in supplying skilled manpower, including Cleaners, Runner Boys, Clerks, Front Desk Officers, and Commercial Executives, to optimize operational efficiency for organizations like DHL Bangladesh. By leveraging third-party talent with the requisite expertise, we ensure cost-effectiveness and seamless operations for our valued clients.
